OUR VISION


Promoting rural tourism and local handicrafts in rural areas are ways to lead India to the path to 'Atmanirbhar Bharat' and make our country self-reliant.

Our website displays the must-visit rural places of India and you will be assigned a local guide. Local residents of these tourist places can register and be a part of the community, hence bringing more employment opportunities to local Indians.

You will get to learn about the history of these places and get an opportunity to view rural art and handicrafts that are prominently highlighted on the website.
