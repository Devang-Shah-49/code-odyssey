MUNSIYARI, UTTARAKHAND

Gujarat is a state in the western part of India famous for its diverse culture, heritage and authentic cuisine. Situated on the banks of Sabarmati River, Gujarat houses some of the best artifacts from the medieval period.

LOCAL HANDICRAFTS

The villages in the vicinity are known to make their livelihood by weaving and they come to Munisiyari to sell their products. Tourists can purchase all kinds of handicrafts and wollen items such as shawls, caps, carpets and more.
